## Design Context

### Users
Young Chinese internet users (18-30) looking for entertainment during breaks, on social media, or when sharing laughs with friends. The job is pure entertainment — a fun distraction with shareable results.

### Brand Personality
Cheeky, organic, delightful. Self-deprecating humor wrapped in a warm, plant-themed aesthetic. Not clinical, not serious — the opposite of a real personality test.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme with organic green palette. Inspired by nature, kawaii aesthetics, and playful plant motifs. Rounded pill shapes, blob forms, leaf decorations. Soft shadows and gentle motion.

Anti-reference: Clinical psychology assessments, corporate personality tests, dark-mode dashboards.

### Design Principles
1. **Organic warmth** — Everything feels soft, rounded, alive (like plants growing)
2. **Entertainment-first** — Every interaction should spark joy or a laugh
3. **Shareable moments** — The result must look good enough to screenshot and share
4. **Mobile-native** — Designed for thumbs first, mouse second
5. **Gentle motion** — Animations that feel natural, like leaves swaying

### Color Palette
- Primary: Sage Green `#87A96B`
- Background: Mint Cream `#F0F7EE`
- Accent: Forest Green `#2E5A3D`
- Text: Dark Charcoal `#2C3E2E`
- Surface: `#FAFDF9`
- Border: `#D4E4D0`

### Typography
- Display: Quicksand (rounded, playful)
- Body: System Chinese stack (PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei, Noto Sans SC)
